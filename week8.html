Final Project Week 8 Journal

This week I started to piece together the storyboards for the interactive story. So far I've sketched out eight frames and have begun putting them together with CSS. I'm happy with the result so far, the aesthetic is coming together nicely. I think the acetates scanned in work really well. The ink doesn't quite settle on the surface of the acetate, their imperfections creating the feel I'm looking for.

New band member! Alexei on keys!

The band have brought in sought after session keyboardist Alexei to help mould the Baltic Funk sound. He brings with him a wealth of experience, a session legend in Russia, he's appeared on countless Russian pop hits. He's also worked as a male model in the UK for Kays catalogue, the handsome brute!

Sprint Goal

For the end of this sprint I'm aiming to have a pinch zoom implemented into the album view, so the user can zoom in and out of the album artwork. I've found a JS library called PinchZoomJS, fingers crossed I get it working. After that I need to look at the anchor points of my draggable objects, during user testing I (and the person who tested the prototype) found that the objects are anchored to the left, when they need to be centred, for a better experience.

Pinch Zoom JS

It's not quite there yet but it's progress. I need to make my imagery smaller and also have a play with the CSS. Nearly there!
